What is Nostr Hero?

Nostr Hero is a nostalgic RPG experience that combines classic D&D 5e mechanics with Nostr's decentralized protocol. Your cryptographic identity deterministically generates a unique adventurer with stats, equipment, and abilities.

The game derives your race, class, background, alignment, and ability scores directly from your Nostr public key. Every player gets a unique character, but the same pubkey always generates the same heroâ€”your identity is your destiny.
